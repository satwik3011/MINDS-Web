About

What is M.I.N.D.S.?

M.I.N.D.S., a not for profit social endeavor, has been created with the thought of giving back to the student community. It is an attempt to create a social impact network that works on the grounds of mutual benefits.


Having known the value of guidance and mentorship, we wish to make correct mentorship easily accessible to all and allow them to fly high in their desired fields. For this, we aim to create a strong and effective mentor-mentee network that caters to different fields of study.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know M.I.N.D.S. by looking at some questions we often recieve:

  • Is M.I.N.D.S. a Startup?

    Unlike a startup or an NGO, M.I.N.D.S. identifies itself as a network working for the welfare and betterment of the student community without any economic motive. It is a student-driven initiative to provide effective mentorship and networking avenues to the students.

  • What does M.I.N.D.S. do?

    M.I.N.D.S. provides for an open platform facilitating one-to-one mentorship within the student community and gives ample opportunities to facilitate growth as well as promote skill development through various engagements, projects and micro-initiatives brought in from time to time.

  • What is the eligibility criteria for mentors?

    The mentor should be a 3rd/4th year undergraduate student or any postgraduate student having relevant skill sets to offer to the mentees.

  • How much time do I need to commit?

    The mentor should be willing to commit a minimum of 1-2 hours per mentee per month with a minimum intake of 3 mentees for 3 months translating to a minimum prescribed commitment of 10-12 hours over the 3 month tenure.

  • What is the eligibility criteria for mentees?

    As a network inclusive of all, M.I.N.D.S. encourages students from all domains, backgrounds and courses to join the community.

  • What can I expect from the initiative?

    Mentees should not misinterpret mentorship with coaching or teaching. Our goal is to provide right guidance and direction to the students and help them get reliable solutions to their problems and dilemmas in an effective manner.
